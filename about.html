THE ROAD TO
EXCELLENCE

AutoApex was born from a simple belief: buying a luxury car should be as exciting as driving one. Founded in 2008 by Arif Rahman in a small showroom in Gulshan, Dhaka, we've grown from a team of 3 to Bangladesh's largest premium car dealership with over 80 dedicated professionals.

We partner directly with BMW, Mercedes-Benz, Audi, Porsche, Lexus, and Land Rover to bring Bangladesh the latest models with full manufacturer backing, warranty support, and genuine parts.

Over 15,000 happy families have driven away in their dream cars thanks to AutoApex. Each one is a story we're proud to have been part of.
